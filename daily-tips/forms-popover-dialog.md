# Popover and dialog
Use native popovers and dialogs for overlays.

```html
<button popovertarget="menu">Open menu</button>
<div id="menu" popover>Popover content</div>

<dialog id="confirm">
  <p>Are you sure?</p>
  <button>OK</button>
</dialog>
```

Why it helps: built-in focus management and accessibility.
